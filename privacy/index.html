  • QR Code Scanning: The app uses your device's camera to scan QR codes for accessing certain features.
  • Google Account Integration: The app allows users to sign in using their Google account for a seamless experience.

2. Permissions Required

To deliver its core functionality, Xplore requests access to the following permissions on your mobile device:

  • Storage Access (Android 12 and below only): Required for saving exported notes to your device's storage.
  • Camera Access: Xplore uses the camera to scan QR codes. This feature is only active when you use the QR code scanning function.

  • Internet Access: Required for accessing Wikipedia content through WikiXplore and other online features.

3. Data Collection and Usage

Xplore takes your privacy seriously and aims to provide transparency in how we collect and use your data.

  • Personal Notes Data: Notes created within the app are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store your notes on our servers. When you export notes, they are saved as text files on your device's storage.
  • Camera Data: The app only uses your camera to scan QR codes. No photos or videos are recorded or saved from your camera unless explicitly mentioned.
  • Google Account Information: Xplore collects the username associated with your Google account when you sign in. This information is used for identification within the app and to enhance your user experience. We do not store passwords or sensitive Google account information.
  • Messaging Data: Messages sent via the app, along with their timestamps, are stored on our servers for up to 24 hours from the time they are sent. After 24 hours, these messages are automatically deleted. If you explicitly uninstall the app, upon reinstallation, the app will fetch any messages that remain within the 24-hour window.

8. Account Deletion

If you choose to delete your account, all personal user information stored on our servers will be permanently deleted. However, your cloud files stored via Google Drive will not be deleted, as they are managed by Google Drive and remain under your control.

You can delete your account and associated data at any time by following these steps:

  1. Open the Xplore app
  2. Navigate to "Account Overview" section
  3. Tap on the "Delete Account" option
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

Once confirmed, all your personal information and data stored on our servers will be permanently removed. If you experience any issues with account deletion, please contact us at wecare@xplore.sbs.
